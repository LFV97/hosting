ABOUT ME

My name is Laura and I have always been passionate about two things that I consider to be very creative, even though they may not look alike: pastry and illustration. I was lucky enough to be able to study both of these things in depth and am now working as a freelance designer. I have worked on book covers and illustrations for children’s books. I love being able to use my creativity to design new products and experiences for people.

In my free time, I like to expand my knowledge by studying web programming or learning other languages like Japanese. I think it is important to never stop learning and pushing yourself creatively, and I hope to continue doing both of these things throughout my life.

One of my favorite things about being a freelance designer is the flexibility it provides. I am able to work from anywhere in the world as long as I have my laptop and internet connection. This has allowed me to travel and experience different cultures while still being able to work and do what I love.
Overall, I am grateful for the opportunities and experiences that have led me to where I am today. I am excited to continue using my creativity in new and innovative ways and to see where my career takes me next.
